2Frequently Asked Questions

🔄 How do I sync my data?

Data synchronization is handled automatically via Apple iCloud (CloudKit). Please ensure that you are signed in to your Apple ID on your device and that iCloud Drive is enabled in your device settings. Your data remains completely private and encrypted.

🛒 How do I restore my Pro purchase?

If you have upgraded to the Pro version but the app is not recognizing it (e.g., after reinstalling or moving to a new device), go to the Settings tab inside the app and tap on Restore Purchase. Make sure you are logged in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

🔒 Is my data safe?

Yes. My BP Diary is designed with extreme privacy in mind. All your health records are stored locally on your device or via your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not operate any external servers and we do not collect, track, or share any of your health data.
